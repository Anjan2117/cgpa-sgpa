How to Calculate CGPA

  1. Go to CGPA Calculator in the navbar.
  2. Click Generate Semester to create a semester starting from Semester 1.
  3. Enter the Number of Credits and SGPA of the respective semesters.
  4. Try to avoid errors. If entered by mistake the input will be cleared for you to enter it again.
  5. Tap Get CGPA to see your result

How to Estimate SGPA

  1. Go to SGPA Estimator in the navbar
  2. Click Generate Semester to create a semester starting from Semester 1.
  3. Enter the Number of Credits and SGPA of the respective semesters.
  4. Enter the Number of Credits and Target/ Estimated SGPA for the upcoming semester
  5. Try to avoid errors. If entered by mistake the input will be cleared for you to enter it again.
  6. Tap Get SGPA to see your result

 

Credits must be a Positive Interger

SGPA/CGPA must be a number from 0 to 10
